Who Are We?

Shelleo Repair Shop is a Computer/Phone Repair business based in East Trinidad that has 10 plus years in the repairs industry. We specialize in fixing and testing mobile phones and computers, providing high quality repairs to our customers. Our Technicians are highly trained with a wealth of experience in repairs. Our aim is to repair all devices we received withing a 2-day mark at an affordable price. We offer options to the customer for what best suits their budget. We take pride in ourselves by offering trusted professional services at Shelleo Repair Shop.
